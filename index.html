About A.

Anna graduated in the top of her class from Nova Scotia College of Art & Design. After college she was selected to intern for the Museum Association of Nova Scotia where she researched artifacts across the province. After learning about museum databases and online archives, Anna followed the history of the tall ships to the Caribbean, where she wrote a successful business plan for a community cafe. On St. Thomas she owned and operated SOS Coffee & Bar Inc. and taught herself to chef for her 48-seat establishment, winning Best Breakfast on St. Thomas the first year in business. The cafe was nearly destroyed by two category 5 hurricanes in 2017. After SOS was rebuilt and reopened, Anna decided that her ambition is creative freedom and mobility. With a deep fondness for the southwest, she moved to Tucson with her two island rescue dogs, and is working on coding and ecommerce ideas.
